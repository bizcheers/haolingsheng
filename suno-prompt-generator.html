How to Use the Prompt Architect

1. Select Your Core Style

Start by picking the vocal gender and tone. Suno works best when you are specific about the "texture" of the voice.

2. Define the Instrumentation

Choose the leading instruments. Don't add too many; 2-3 specific instruments generate a cleaner mix than a generic list.

3. Layer the Atmosphere

Adding a mood like "Cinematic" or "Nostalgic" helps Suno choose the right reverb and spatial processing.

4. Control the Song Form

Use structural tags like [Chorus] or [Drop] to guide the AI's internal progression. These are essential for longer tracks.
