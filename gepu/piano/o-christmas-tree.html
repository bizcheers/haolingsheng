📖 Historical Background

O Christmas Tree (O Tannenbaum) — 'O Tannenbaum' is a German folk song whose melody dates to the 16th century, with modern lyrics by Ernst Anschütz (1824). Its stately 3/4 character and singable melody have made it one of the most enduring European Christmas songs.

Composer: German Traditional (16th century).

🔬 Musical Structure

In 3/4 time with a dignified, waltz-like character. The A-A-B-A phrase structure flows naturally, with the B section providing a brief moment of contrast. The two-voice piano texture adds harmonic richness.

🎵 Piano Performance Tips

  • The 3/4 feel should be stately and measured, not rushed.
  • Give each phrase a natural arc—balance starting quietly and growing slightly toward the midpoint.
  • The left hand's steady quarter notes underpin the whole arrangement.
