The Legend Behind the Hits

Andre Romelle Young, known professionally as Dr. Dre, is an American rapper, record producer, and entrepreneur. He is the founder and CEO of Aftermath Entertainment and Beats Electronics.

  • My Name Is - Eminem

Impact on Music Industry

Dre not only created the G-Funk sound but also discovered diverse talents like Eminem and 50 Cent, proving he has the best ear for talent in the game.
