Instrument: Trumpet Key: G Major Level: Beginner

About This Arrangement

This beginner trumpet arrangement of "We Wish You a Merry Christmas" is transposed to G Major (written C Major for Trumpet) to ensure all notes stay comfortably within the lower register. It covers exactly one octave, making it perfect for students who have just mastered their first scale.

Learning Goals

  • Mastering the 3/4 time signature (Waltz feel).
  • Practicing consistent breath control on longer musical phrases.
  • Developing finger dexterity for the traditional Christmas melody.

Beginner Trumpet Tips

When playing this festive tune, focus on the "pick-up" notes. The melody starts on the 3rd beat of a measure, so count carefully before starting!
