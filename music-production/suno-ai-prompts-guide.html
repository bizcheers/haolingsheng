Speaking the Language of V3

Suno V3 is incredible, but like any AI, it needs clear instructions. Just typing "Pop Song" will give you generic trash. To get professional results, you need to use Style Tags and Structural Metatags.

Essential Metatags

Place these tags inside your lyrics box to control the song flow:

  • [Intro] - Starts the song with an instrumental build-up.
  • [Verse] - Tells the AI to sing in a storytelling cadence.
  • [Chorus] - Enhances the energy and creates a hook.
  • [Guitar Solo] - Forces a solo section (works great!).
  • [Outro] - Fades the song out.
