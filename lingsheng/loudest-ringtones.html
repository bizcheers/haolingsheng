How We Make Them "Loud"

Volume is relative. To make a ringtone "unmissable," we don't just turn up the gain; we focus on the **1kHz - 5kHz frequency range**. This is the area where the human ear is naturally most sensitive. By using sawtooth waves and aggressive EQ in this "presence" region, these tones sound 2-3 times louder than standard music.
