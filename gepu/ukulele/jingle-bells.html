About This Arrangement

This beginner ukulele arrangement of "Jingle Bells" features both standard music notation and specialized ukulele tablature. It is arranged in the key of C Major and uses standard GCEA tuning. The melody is kept on the higher strings (E and A) to allow for a bright, cheerful Christmas sound.

Learning Goals

  • Understanding GCEA ukulele tablature.
  • Practicing consistent down-strums or fingerpicking on melodic notes.
  • Learning to read simple rhythmic values (quarter and eighth notes).

Ukulele Tab Tip

Ukulele tabs have 4 lines, representing the four strings. The top line is the A string (highest pitch), and the bottom line is the G string (standard re-entrant tuning).
