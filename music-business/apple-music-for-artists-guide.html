The Premium Platform

Apple Music is known for its high-fidelity audio and its high payout-per-stream relative to competitors. For independent artists, the Apple Music for Artists dashboard provides some of the most beautiful and insightful data in the music industry. It helps you see your career in a global context.

How to Claim Your Profile

  1. Visit the Portal: Go to artists.apple.com.
  2. Sign In: Use your personal Apple ID.
  3. Search for Yourself: Find your artist name in the database.
  4. Verification: Like Spotify, Apple will ask for proof of identity via your distributor or social media accounts.
  5. Review Time: Apple's verification team typically takes 3-7 days to approve new claims.

The Most Powerful Features

  • Global Listening Maps: Apple's map is incredibly detailed. You can see not just which countries are listening, but which specific cities. This is essential for planning tours!
  • Shazam Insights: Since Apple owns Shazam, you can see exactly when and where people are "Shazam-ing" your music. This is one of the best ways to track when your music gets played on radio or in public places.
  • Milestone Badges: When you hit 10k streams in a week or 100 library saves, Apple provides clean, shareable social media assets to celebrate with your fans.
  • Profile Customization: Change your artist image to ensure your brand looks premium across the Apple ecosystem.

Apple Music vs. Spotify Analytics

While Spotify is great for real-time data, Apple Music is excellent for long-term trend analysis. Their "Listen Now" section gives you a summary of your most active cities and tracks, helping you make high-level business decisions about where to spend your marketing budget.
