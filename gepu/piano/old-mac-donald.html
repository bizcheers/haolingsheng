About This Arrangement

This beginner piano arrangement of "Old MacDonald Had a Farm" is set in C Major to minimize the need for black keys. It features a simplified right-hand melody and basic harmonic support in the left hand (using C and G chords), making it a perfect early piece for young kids.

Learning Goals

  • Mastering repeated note rhythms (E-I-E-I-O).
  • Coordinating simple chord changes in the left hand.
  • Developing rhythmic consistency with quarter and eighth notes.

🎹 Practice Tips

  • Take it slow at first. Accuracy is more important than speed.
  • Focus on a steady rhythm and clear articulation.
  • Use a metronome to keep your tempo consistent.

Piano Tip

When playing "Old MacDonald," focus on the "E-I-E-I-O" section. It's a great opportunity to practice finger independence on the C Major five-finger position.
