Not Replacement, But Inspiration

Using AI to write a whole song usually results in cheesy poetry. But using AI to brainstorm is a superpower. Think of ChatGPT (or Claude) as a co-writer who never gets tired and has read every dictionary in existence.

Prompt: "I have a basic chord progression: C - G - Am - F. Give me 3 jazzier variations using 7th chords and secondary dominants to make it sound more like Neo-Soul."

üìù Rhyme Architect

Prompt: "Give me a list of near-rhymes (slant rhymes) for the word 'Fire'. Do not give me perfect rhymes like 'Desire' or 'Higher'. Focus on assonance."

Structure Breaker

Stuck in Verse-Chorus-Verse? Ask AI: "Generate 3 unconventional song structures used in Progressive House music." It might suggest a "Double Drop" or a "Post-Chorus Breakdown" that sparks your next arrangement idea.
