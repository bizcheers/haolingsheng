Quincy Jones

The Titan of Music

The Legend Behind the Hits

Quincy Delight Jones Jr. is an American record producer, musician, songwriter, composer, arranger, and film and television producer. His career spans over 70 years in the entertainment industry.

Quincy's sound is characterized by lush orchestration, impeccable jazz-influenced arrangements, and a groove that is undeniable. He is a master of blending genres, from big band to funk and pop.

Notable Productions

Some of the most iconic tracks produced by Quincy Jones include:

  • Billie Jean - Michael Jackson
  • Thriller - Michael Jackson
  • We Are the World - USA for Africa
  • Fly Me to the Moon - Frank Sinatra (Arrangement)
  • Give Me the Night - George Benson

Impact on Music Industry

Producing 'Thriller', the best-selling album of all time, cements his legacy. He broke down racial barriers in the music industry and has won 28 Grammy Awards.
