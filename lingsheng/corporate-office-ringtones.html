The Science of "Non-Intrusive" Alerts

What makes a sound "professional"? Audio psychologists point to two factors: Attack Time and Frequency Banding. Harsh alarms often have an instant attack (0ms), which triggers the human startle response. Our corporate tones feature a Linear Fade-In of 10ms to 50ms, making the sound feel like it's "appearing" rather than "striking."

Furthermore, we avoid the 3kHz to 5kHz range where the human ear is most sensitive to pain. Instead, we focus on the 800Hz to 1.5kHz range, which is easily audible over the hum of office HVAC systems but doesn't feel aggressive. This is the same principle of acoustic clarity we discuss in our Zen & Calm series.
