The "Co-Writer" Approach

ChatGPT should not be seen as a replacement for the songwriter, but as a tireless co-writer. Instead of asking it to "write a song," engage in a dialogue to refine specific elements. This prevents the "AI-generic" feel and keeps your unique voice at the center.

Creative Prompting Strategies

Use these prompts to break through specific creative blocks:

  • Metaphor Deep-Dive: "Generate 10 metaphors for 'unanswered love' that use architectural and urban planning terminology."
  • Rhyme Scheme Variation: "I have a verse with an AABB scheme. Rewrite it using an ABAB scheme while keeping the theme of 'nighttime solitude'."
  • Narrative Arc: "Outline a 3-act story for a folk song about a clockmaker who loses his sense of time."

Generating Musical Structures

ChatGPT is excellent at suggesting chord progressions based on emotional theory.

Example Prompt: "Suggest a chord progression for a melancholic indie-rock bridge that starts on the IV chord and builds tension using a diminished chord before resolving to the V."
