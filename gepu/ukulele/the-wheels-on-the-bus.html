📖 Historical Background

The Wheels on the Bus — 'The Wheels on the Bus' was written by Verna Hills and published in 1939 in the 'American Singer' songbook. It is now one of the most widely sung children's songs in the English-speaking world, beloved for its repetitive, action-driven verses.

Composer: Verna Hills (1939).

🔬 Musical Structure

The song uses a simple, singable melody in 4/4 time that stays within a narrow range, making it perfectly suited for beginners. Each verse follows the same melodic pattern with different lyrics, reinforcing rhythm and structure.

🎵 Ukulele Performance Tips

  • Keep a steady, bouncy strum pattern to match the playful character.
  • Sing along as you play to help lock in the rhythm.
  • Emphasise beats 1 and 3 to give the song a 'bus-like' momentum.
