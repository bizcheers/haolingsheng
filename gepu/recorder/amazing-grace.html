📖 Historical Background

Amazing Grace — 'Amazing Grace' was written by English poet and clergyman John Newton and published in 1779. Newton wrote it as a reflection on his spiritual journey after having worked in the slave trade. It became one of the most recognisable hymns in the English-speaking world.

Composer: John Newton (1779).

🔬 Musical Structure

In 3/4 time with long, flowing phrases. The melody stays in a comfortable range for the recorder and uses smooth stepwise movement. The emotional arc of the melody builds gradually from the opening to a climactic phrase before resolving peacefully.

🎵 Recorder Performance Tips

  • Use a gentle, steady breath support to sustain the long phrases.
  • Avoid over-blowing—the recorder responds best to a focused, moderate breath.
  • The 3/4 feel should have a gentle pulse, never mechanical.
