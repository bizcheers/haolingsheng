📖 Historical Background

We Shall Overcome — 'We Shall Overcome' evolved from the gospel song 'I'll Overcome Someday' (1900) and became the anthem of the American civil rights movement in the 1960s. In 2017, a US court ruled the song is in the public domain. The recorder's pure, simple tone gives this deeply meaningful melody a particularly moving quality.

Composer: Traditional Spiritual (public domain as of 2017)

🔬 Musical Structure

In 4/4 time with a slow, dignified character. The melody is built on long, sustained notes with simple stepwise movement. The limited range makes it very accessible for beginner recorder players.

🎵 Recorder Performance Tips

  • Play with a slow, steady breath and a focused, gentle tone — the power of this piece comes from its simplicity and dignity.
  • The sustained notes must be supported with a consistent, unwavering breath. Avoid any wavering in tone.
  • Play as one long, connected phrase wherever possible. The legato connection between notes gives the melody its emotional depth.
