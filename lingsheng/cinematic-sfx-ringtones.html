The Power of Sound Design

Cinematic audio isn't just about loudness; it's about **frequency management** and **stereo depth**. We use sub-bass impacts, orchestral risers, and metallic textures to create alerts that feel expensive. Unlike generic "beeps," these sounds have a beginning, a climax, and a natural decay.

Behind the Sound

Ever heard of the **Shepard Tone**? It's an auditory illusion of a sound that continually ascends or descends in pitch but ultimately seems to get no higher or lower. We use elements of this in our "Infinite Risers" collection to create a sense of mounting excitement as your phone rings.
