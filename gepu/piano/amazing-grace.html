About This Arrangement

This beginner piano arrangement of "Amazing Grace" is set in F Major (one flat) to introduce students to the first key signature beyond C Major. The left hand provides simple, supportive harmonies (F, Bb, and C7 chords) while the right hand carries the iconic pentatonic melody.

Learning Goals

  • Learning the Bb key signature and its placement on the keyboard.
  • Coordinating simple harmonic support in the left hand.
  • Practicing melodic phrasing and expressive dynamics.

Piano Practice Tip

Visualize the "one-flat" (Bb) as the only black key you'll need for this piece. Keep your left-hand movements small and steady to maintain a solid foundation.
