What is a Transient Shaper?

Unlike a compressor that works based on a threshold, a **transient shaper VST** detects the "initial hit" (the attack) of a sound regardless of its volume. This makes it the most effective tool for making a kick drum knock or a snare snap without ruining the dynamics of the performance.

The Two Magic Knobs

  • Attack: Boosting this adds "crack" and "punch." Reducing it makes sounds feel softer and more distant.
  • Sustain: Boosting this brings out the tail or the "room" sound. Reducing it makes elements like claps or hi-hats sharp and dry.
