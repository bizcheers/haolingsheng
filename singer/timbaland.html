The Beat Magician

Timbaland

Timothy Zachary Mosley, known professionally as Timbaland, is an American record producer, rapper, singer, and songwriter. He has received widespread acclaim for his innovative production work with Missy Elliott, Aaliyah, and Justin Timberlake.

Signature Sound

Timbaland's signature sound involves complex, syncopated drum patterns, beatboxing used as percussion, and creative use of foreign samples and vocal grunts. He created a futuristic sound that defined the 2000s.
