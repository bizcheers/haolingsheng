📖 Historical Background

The Wheels on the Bus — 'The Wheels on the Bus' was written by Verna Hills and published in 1939. Now beloved worldwide as a children's song, its repetitive structure and simple melody make it an ideal piece for beginner guitarists to develop rhythm and coordination.

Composer: Verna Hills (1939).

🔬 Musical Structure

The melody sits in a comfortable open position and uses simple rhythmic patterns. The tab shows how to play the melody on the lower strings, where the guitar's warmth of tone complements the song's character.

🎵 Guitar Performance Tips

  • Practice the melody slowly until the left-hand fingerings feel comfortable.
  • Keep a steady up-down strumming pattern when playing the rhythm accompaniment.
  • Sing along to help internalise the form.
