📖 Historical Background

Jingle Bells — 'Jingle Bells' was composed by James Lord Pierpont and published in 1857. It was the first song broadcast from space (1965, Gemini 6). Originally written for Thanksgiving, it has since become one of the most recognised Christmas songs in the world.

Composer: James Lord Pierpont (1857).

🔬 Musical Structure

The melody features a catchy, energetic refrain that repeats several times, making it ideal for memorisation. It is in 4/4 time with predominantly eighth-note movement that gives it a driving, joyful feel.

🎵 Ukulele Performance Tips

  • Use a fast down-up strum for the chorus to capture the driving energy.
  • The verse can be played with a slower, more deliberate strum.
  • Make sure the chord transitions are smooth—practice G to C and G to D7 until they feel natural.

Ukulele Tab Tip

Ukulele tabs have 4 lines, representing the four strings. The top line is the A string (highest pitch), and the bottom line is the G string (standard re-entrant tuning).
