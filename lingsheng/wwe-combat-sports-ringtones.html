The Science of Power Ringtones

Entrance themes are unique because they are designed to be heard over a screaming crowd. This makes them scientifically superior to standard pop songs as ringtones. They utilize Mid-Range Aggression—fiddles, electric guitars, and heavy synths—that sit right in the frequency band where the human brain detects emergency signals.

If you're using our Android Mastery guide to set these up, remember that you can often use FLAC files on modern Android devices to preserve the transient "kick" of these high-energy tracks.

Remastering for Mobile

We apply a specialized Limiter chain to all our Combat Sports files. By squashing the peaks and bringing up the low-level details, we ensure that the "atmosphere" of the entrance is maintained, even when the handset is buried in a bag or pocket. This is similar to the approach we take for our Loudest Ringtones collection.
