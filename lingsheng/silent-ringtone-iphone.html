Silent Ringtone for iPhone: The Ultimate Focus Hack

Want to ignore a contact without them knowing they're blocked? A silent ringtone is your best friend. Download it here.

Why Use a Silent Ringtone?

While "Do Not Disturb" and "Silence Unknown Callers" are great globally, sometimes you want to target specific individuals. Blocking someone can be drastic and sometimes detectable. By assigning a Silent Ringtone to a specific contact, your phone will stay completely quiet when they call, while still allowing you to see the notification or missed call later.

How to Set This Up on iPhone

Follow these steps to "mute" a specific contact using your new silent file:

  1. Add to Library: Follow our Custom Ringtone Guide to add this silent WAV to your iPhone's ringtone list.
  2. Open Contacts: Find the person you want to silence.
  3. Edit Contact: Tap "Edit" in the top right corner.
  4. Assign Ringtone: Scroll down to "Ringtone" and select your new Silent file.
  5. Save: Tap "Done". Now, when they call, your phone won't vibrate or make a sound.

Common Use Cases

  • Persistent Telemarketers: If they use a consistent number, silence them permanently.
  • Work Boundaries: Silence work colleagues after hours without affecting family calls.
  • Custom Alarm Delay: Use silence at the start of a mixed audio file to create a "delayed" alarm experience.

Vibration Tip

Don't forget to also set the Vibration to "None" for that contact if you want total stealth. You'll find this setting right below the Ringtone selection in the Edit Contact menu.
