What is Stable Audio?

Stable Audio is a latent diffusion model specifically trained for high-quality audio generation. While Suno and Udio focus on songs, Stable Audio is a favorite for creating ambient soundscapes, drum loops, and sound effects.

Advanced Audio Prompting

To get the best results, use technical audio terms. For example:

  • "4/4 Techno kick drum, 130 BPM, saturated, analog character"
  • "Cinematic ambient swell, reverb-heavy, shimmering textures, 44.1kHz"
