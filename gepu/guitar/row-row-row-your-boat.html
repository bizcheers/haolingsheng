📖 Historical Background

Row, Row, Row Your Boat — 'Row, Row, Row Your Boat' was first published in 1852. It is one of the most famous children's songs in the English language and is often performed as a round (canon). The guitar arrangement showcases the melody and makes it easy to learn the tune.

Composer: Traditional (1852).

🔬 Musical Structure

In 6/8 time with a gentle, rocking character reminiscent of rowing. The melody uses mainly stepwise motion and is structured as a simple single-verse round. The tab keeps the arrangement in first position for accessibility.

🎵 Guitar Performance Tips

  • The 6/8 feel should be smooth and rocking—count '1-2-3 4-5-6' and feel the two main beats.
  • Use a gentle picking style to complement the song's peaceful character.
