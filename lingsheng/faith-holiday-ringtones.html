Why Seasonal Ringtones?

Seasonal ringtones are a great way to "refresh" your relationship with your device. Psychologically, changing your phone's sound every few months reduces "notification fatigue" and makes the device feel new again.

Production Note: The Organ & Bell EQ

Organ and Bell sounds often have very high-frequency overtones that can sound "piercing" on cheap phone speakers. We've applied a **High-Shelf attenuation** to our Christian and Holiday packs to ensure they sound smooth and professional, never "tinny".
