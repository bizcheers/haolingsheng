About This Arrangement

This beginner arrangement of "Happy Birthday" for Alto Saxophone is transposed to G Major (written C Major for Sax) to ensure all notes stay within the most accessible range for new players. It's a fundamental piece for any student learning the basics of woodwind fingerings.

Learning Goals

  • Mastering the 3/4 time signature and pick-up notes.
  • Practicing smooth transitions between the left and right hand keys.
  • Developing a consistent, warm saxophone tone on familiar melodies.

🎹 Practice Tips

  • Take it slow at first. Accuracy is more important than speed.
  • Focus on a steady rhythm and clear articulation.
  • Use a metronome to keep your tempo consistent.

Saxophone Tip

When playing "Happy Birthday," remember that the first note is a pick-up. Take a deep breath and start clearly on the 3rd beat of the imaginary starting measure!
