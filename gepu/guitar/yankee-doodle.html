📖 Historical Background

Yankee Doodle — 'Yankee Doodle' is a well-known American song from the 18th century, with origins in pre-Revolutionary War America. The melody likely derives from an older British or Dutch song. It is the state anthem of Connecticut and a symbol of American patriotism.

🔬 Musical Structure

The melody is in 4/4 time with a lively, march-like character. It uses eighth-note movement and clear four-bar phrases that repeat predictably. The tab sits entirely in first position, making it very accessible for beginners.
