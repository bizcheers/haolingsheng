📖 Historical Background

Silent Night — Originally performed on guitar and voice at a small church in Austria in 1818, 'Silent Night' has since become the world's most recorded Christmas song. Its simple, lullaby-like melody is beautifully suited to the gentle character of the recorder.

Composer: Franz Xaver Gruber (1818).

🔬 Musical Structure

The 6/8 time signature creates a gentle, rocking feel. Notes are predominantly quarter and eighth lengths, staying in a comfortable upper range for the recorder. The symmetrical phrases make it easy to memorise.

🎵 Recorder Performance Tips

  • Use a gentle breath and avoid over-blowing to keep the tone soft and warm.
  • The 6/8 feel should be smooth and lilting, never rushing.
  • Breathe at the end of each four-bar phrase.
